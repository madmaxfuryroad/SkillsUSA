Meet the Fashion Devas

Jennie Kim

Jennie Kim, the talented and stylish member of the internationally renowned K-pop group BLACKPINK, has taken the fashion world by storm as she becomes the face of Chanel. Renowned for her impeccable fashion sense and captivating stage presence, Jennie's collaboration with Chanel brings together two icons of elegance and sophistication. As Chanel's brand ambassador, Jennie's youthful charm and effortless grace perfectly complement the luxury fashion house's timeless designs. With her striking beauty and dynamic charisma, Jennie Kim effortlessly embodies Chanel's ethos of modernity and innovation while respecting the brand's classic heritage. Her impeccable taste and ability to effortlessly pull off any ensemble make her an ideal choice to represent Chanel's diverse range of collections. Jennie's partnership with Chanel not only strengthens the brand's appeal to a global audience but also showcases the seamless fusion of K-pop and high fashion, further solidifying her position as a trendsetter and style icon in the fashion industry. As Jennie continues to grace the runways and campaigns of Chanel, her collaboration with the iconic brand is set to leave an indelible mark on the fashion world for years to come.

Lily Rose Depp

Lily-Rose Depp, the daughter of Hollywood superstar Johnny Depp and French singer Vanessa Paradis, has emerged as a rising star in the world of fashion, particularly as a muse for Chanel. With her innate poise and exquisite beauty, Lily-Rose effortlessly captures the essence of Chanel's elegance and allure. Her ethereal features and sartorial flair make her the perfect choice to represent the iconic brand, as she effortlessly embodies the spirit of modernity and sophistication that Chanel is renowned for. As a model and ambassador for Chanel, Lily-Rose Depp has graced the runways and adorned the pages of prestigious fashion magazines, leaving an indelible mark on the industry. Her captivating presence and undeniable talent have garnered her a dedicated following of fashion enthusiasts and admirers worldwide. Chanel's association with Lily-Rose further cements the brand's reputation for choosing trailblazing talent and showcases its commitment to celebrating the diversity and beauty of contemporary femininity. With her star on the rise and her partnership with Chanel flourishing, Lily-Rose Depp continues to be a source of inspiration and admiration, breathing new life into the legacy of one of the most iconic fashion houses in the world.

Kristan Stewart

Kristen Stewart, the acclaimed actress known for her versatility and unique sense of style, has made a seamless transition into the world of high fashion as a model for Chanel. As the face of the iconic brand, Kristen exudes a captivating mix of edginess and elegance, perfectly embodying Chanel's spirit of modernity and sophistication. With her enigmatic allure and fearless approach to fashion, she brings a fresh perspective to the brand's campaigns and runway shows, captivating audiences and fashion enthusiasts alike. Chanel's collaboration with Kristen Stewart showcases the brand's commitment to embracing individuality and celebrating the multi-faceted nature of contemporary women. Known for her exceptional acting skills and her ability to fully immerse herself in diverse roles, Kristen's modeling career with Chanel is a natural extension of her artistic expression. Her magnetic presence and ability to effortlessly carry off any ensemble make her a captivating muse for the renowned fashion house, elevating their designs to new heights. As Kristen continues to make her mark as a model for Chanel, she leaves an indelible impression on the fashion world, proving that her talent and charisma extend beyond the silver screen and into the realm of high fashion.
