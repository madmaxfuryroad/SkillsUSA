  • Time: About 45 minutes
  • Rating: 5

To make pasta, start by boiling a pot of water. Add salt to the water to enhance the pasta's flavor. Once the water is boiling, carefully add the pasta and stir immediately to prevent sticking. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package, usually for around 8-12 minutes. While the pasta is cooking, you can prepare the sauce or toppings of your choice. Once the pasta is al dente, meaning it's cooked but still slightly firm, drain it in a colander. Rinse the pasta with a little cold water to stop the cooking process. Then, combine the pasta with your desired sauce or toppings, and toss everything together. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pasta!
